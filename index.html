Project Example 1

Businesses' Electricity Bill Determinants

Mexico city's businesses consume lots of Electricity, specially when their activity is related to water consumption, machinery and a bigger number of employees.

The impact of these three variables is differentiated by business activity and size.

However in general the longer hours employees stay at the location the higher the electricity bill, followed by far by the machinery intensity and water consumption.

This project was included into a Market study categorizing and locating the businesses in a map classifying city blocks in order to plan the Marketing strategy of a Solar Power Provider

Project 2

Cancellations and holiday's impact over dayli trips

Motorcycles are used as a mean of transportation in Africa. Safe boda is a safe trip app that has a branch in Nairobi, Kenya.

Motorcycle travelling is not as popular during the holidays and Safe Boda wanted to know how imprtant the impact was.

The East African capital shows two different rush hours a day and travelling is most pupular during the night.

Cancellation rates are highest at night when drivers take longer to arrive, people do not like to wait at dangerous hours.
